# Colto Privacy Policy

[English](index.md) | [Español](index-es.md)

**Last updated: 7/18/2026

Colto is a fast, lightweight dictionary app that respects your privacy. This policy explains what information the app does — and does not — collect.

---

## Information We Collect
Colto **does not collect any personal information** from you.

The app does not request your name, email, phone number, location, contacts, or any other personal data.

---

## Dictionary Data
Colto stores dictionary entries and search results **locally on your device** so they can be accessed offline.  
This information is not linked to you and cannot be used to identify you.

---

## Search Terms
When you look up a word that is not yet stored on your device, Colto may perform an **optional online lookup**.

In this case, the app sends **only the search term** to a dictionary service to retrieve the definition.  
No personal information is sent, stored, or shared.

---

## Internet Access
Colto works fully offline.  
Internet access is used **only** for optional online lookups of words not yet available locally.

---

## Data Sharing
Colto **does not share any data** with third parties.  
We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking tools, or external data collectors.

---

## Children’s Privacy
Colto does not collect personal data and is safe for users of all ages.

---

## Your Choices
You can use Colto entirely offline.  
If you prefer not to use online lookup, simply keep your device offline — the app will continue to function normally.
